Medium Firm

The EcoSanctuary Firm mattress provides an organic sleep retreat for those requiring firm support.The firm Talalay latex layer provides resilient support to ensure correct spinal alignment and ease stress. The all-natural Dunlop latex core made of USDA certified organically grown latex offers equalized, targeted support to individual pressure points for delicious comfort. Certified 100% organic Natura Grow Wool™ wicks away moisture to create a dry, temperature regulated sleep haven while it eliminates harmful bacteria, dust-mites and other sleep disturbing allergens. Dressed with a deliciously soft, 100% certified organic cotton stretch knit ticking, this firm organic mattress offers you a healthful organic haven to rest and rejuvenate.
